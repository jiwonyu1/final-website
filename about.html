Jiwon (she/her) is a maker of new medium art based in New York City. Her main exploration points in art include sexual conflicts, womenhood, and cultural identities, which derives from her personal experiences. Jiwon enjoys stemming her projects from traditional art mediums, such as painting, drawing, classical violin, then adding an interactive element by incorporating a layer of technology. She graduates from NYU Tisch's IMA (Interactive Media Arts) program in 2024.

Jiwon was born in South Korea, and moved to the United States during her pre-collegiate years. Her favorite dog breed is chihuahua and standard poodle.
